Aroma Chemicals

Aroma chemicals are a class of organic compounds that are used to impart fragrance to products. They are typically derived from natural sources, such as plants, fruits, and other aromatic materials. Aroma chemicals are used in a wide range of products, including perfumes, cosmetics, soaps, and household cleaners. They are also used in the food and beverage industry to add flavor and aroma to products.
